Who is Escape Poverty(EP)-Africa

Established in December 2013, Escape Poverty (EP) - Africa is a non-governmental, non-profit organization operating in Ghana. EP-Africa works to spur social and economic development through capacity building, Technical Assistance (TA), Entrepreneurial Skills and Enterprises Development targeting the youth (15-25 years) and women. Our Theory of Change is that if the youth and women in particularly become socially and economically empowered they are better able to change the world, making it a better place for all.



Vision

Inclusive socio-economic development, wealth and prosperity for all people.



Mission

To socially and economically empower the youth particularly women with requisite tools; skills, knowledge and attitudes through capacity building and creation of opportunities for them to act as effective change agents in their communities



Objectives

To socially and economically empower 6,000 youth (30% women) to enable them lead healthy, fulfilling lives of self-reliance and dignity by 2022.



OUR CORE VALUES

The core values which guide and govern the organization’s daily interactions with customers and industry stakeholders are:

  • -Integrity

  • -Professionalism

  • -Respect for Diversity



KEY FOCUS AREAS

  • -Renewable Energy and Environment

  • -Youth, Women Economic and Social Empowerment

  • -Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs)

  • -Agriculture and Agribusiness

  • -Extractives sector (Mining, Oil & Gas)

  • -Public Sector
